## THE VISION

SkyForge is a teen metaverse flight academy where gameplay and career are the same thing.

Two modes. One platform. One continuous world.

**PLAY MODE — Red Arrows Formation Flying**
Social, multiplayer, VR-first. Kids fly the Red Arrows in formation with friends. Chaotic, rewatchable, competitive. Designed to compete with Roblox on its own terms — immersive, social, and haptic.

**CAREER MODE — Licensed Flight Academy**
The same aircraft, the same world — but now your hours count. XP converts to rank. Rank converts to real-world pathway credentials. Aimed at 14–18 year olds who want the Red Arrows feeling to mean something.

---

## THE PROBLEM

Flight simulation exists (MSFS, X-Plane). Gaming exists (Roblox, Fortnite). Career pathways exist (flight school). **Nothing connects all three for young people.**

Roblox has no real-world destination. Flight simulators have no social layer. Flight schools have no entry point for teenagers. SkyForge is the bridge.

---

## THE RETENTION ARC

Age 8-12 → Age 13-14 → Age 15-18 → Age 18+
Play mode / Red Arrows → Discovers career track → Career mode XP = credentials → Real flight school referral

This is a retention arc Roblox cannot replicate — because Roblox has no real-world destination.

## TARGET MARKET

**Primary:** 13–18 year olds interested in aviation, gaming, or both  
**Secondary:** 8–12 year olds via social Play mode (Red Arrows)  
**Tertiary:** Flight schools seeking a digital recruitment funnel  

**Market size:**
- 3.2 billion gamers worldwide
- Aviation industry projects a shortage of 600,000+ pilots by 2040 (Boeing forecast)
- No dominant teen-facing flight education platform exists

## TECHNOLOGY ROADMAP

### PHASE 1 — Browser MVP (COMPLETE ✅)
- Cinematic onboarding with callsign registration
- Track selection (Commercial / Fighter)
- Mission brief and career preview
- Stack: React, deployed on Vercel

### PHASE 2 — Cockpit + Career Dashboard (COMPLETE ✅)
- Live animated instrument panel (AH, ASI, ALT, VSI)
- Mission board with XP and SkyBucks rewards
- Career progression with rank roadmap
- Economy store with SkyBucks transactions
- Squad panel with leaderboard and invite system
- Flight log

### PHASE 3 — Meta Quest VR (SEEKING UNITY DEVELOPER)
- Full 3D cockpit environment in Unity
- Red Arrows multiplayer formation mode
- Meta XR SDK integration
- Same accounts, same XP, same SkyBucks across platforms
- Social voice comms in formation

### PHASE 4 — Haptic Layer (FUTURE)
- bHaptics / Shiftall suit integration
- G-force simulation on manoeuvres
- Turbulence, carrier trap thud, cannon fire
- **This is the product that gets press, gets school partnerships, gets flight school attention**
